To express going to do verb, change the verb into it's [conjunctive form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjunctive/)
and add 'ㄹ 줄 알다/모르다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Know How |
| 썰다 | to chop/slice | 썰줄 알다 |
| 말하다 | to talk | 말할줄 알다 |
| 하다 | to do | 할줄 알아 |
