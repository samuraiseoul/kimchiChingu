To express 'even if verb, ...' change the verb into it's [conjugate form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjugate/)
and add '도'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Even if |
| 입다 | to wear  | 입어도 |
| 자르다 | to cut | 잘라도 |
| 후회하다 | to regret | 후회해도 |
