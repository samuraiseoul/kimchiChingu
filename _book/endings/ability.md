To use the ability form of a verb, change the verb into it's [conjunctive form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjunctive/)
and add 'ㄹ 수 있다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Ability |
| 시작하다 | to start | 시작항수있다 |
| 고치다 | to fix/heal | 고칠수있다 |
| 놓다 | to place | 놓을수있다 |
