To use the past tense form of a verb, change the verb into it's [conjugate form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjugate/)
and add 'ㅆ다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Past |
| 나누다 | to divide | 나눴다 |
| 헤어지다 | to separate | 헤어졌다 |
| 묻다 | to ask | 묻었다 |
