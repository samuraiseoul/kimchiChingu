To use the suggestive form(let's do) of a verb, change the verb into it's [stem form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/stem/)
and add '자'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Suggestive |
| 무시하다 | to ignore | 무시하자 |
| 듣다 | to listen | 듣자 |
| 따라가다 | to follow | 따라가자 |
