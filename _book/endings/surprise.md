To express going to do verb, change the verb into it's [stem form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/stem/)
and add '네'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Surprise |
| 알다 | to know | 알네 |
| 자다 | to sleep | 자네 |
| 모르다 | to not know | 모르네 |
