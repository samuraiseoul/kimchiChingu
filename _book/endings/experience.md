To express that one must do something or or has to do something, change the
verb into it's [conjugate form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjugate/) and
add '본 적 있다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Experienced |
| 요리하다 | to cook | 요리해본적있다 |
| 느끼다 | to feel | 느껴본적있다 |
| 맞다 | to be hit | 맞아본적있다 |
