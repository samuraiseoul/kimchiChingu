To say that you want to do verb, change the verb into it's [stem form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/stem/)
and add '고싶다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Want |
| 보내다 | to send  | 보내고싶다 |
| 변하다 | to change | 변하고싶다 |
| 놀다 | to play | 놀고싶다 |
