To use the present progressive form of a verb, change the verb into it's [stem form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/stem/)
and add '고 있다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Give/Receive |
| 보다 | to look/see | 보고있다 |
| 올라가다 | to go up | 올라가고있다 |
| 생각하다 | to think | 생각하고있다 |
