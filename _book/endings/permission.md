To ask for permission to do change the verb into it's [conjugate form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjugate/)
and add '도 되다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Permission |
| 노래하다 | to sing  | 노래해도되다 |
| 빌리다 | to borrow | 빌려도되다 |
| 섞다 | to mix | 섞어도되다 |
