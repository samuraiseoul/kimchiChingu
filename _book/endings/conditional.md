To use the conditional form of a verb, change the verb into it's [conjunctive form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjunctive/)
and add '면'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Give/Receive |
| 기억하다 | to remember | 기억하면 |
| 치다 | to hit | 치면 |
| 갖다 | to have | 갖으면 |
