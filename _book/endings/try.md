To use the experience form of a verb, change the verb into it's [conjugate form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjugate/)
and add '보다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Give/Receive |
| 말하다 | to say | 말해보다 |
| 서다 | to stand | 서보다 |
| 잡다 | to grab | 잡아보다 |
