To use the favor form of a verb, change the verb into it's [conjugate form]({{site.baseurl}}/book/verbs/conjugate/)
and add '주다'.

### Examples:

| Dictionary | English | Give/Receive |
| 태우다 | to burn | 태워주다 |
| 지키다 | to protect | 지켜주다 |
| 사다 | to buy | 사주다 |
