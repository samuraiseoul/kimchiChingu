Stems Ending in Consonants

Verb and adjective stems ending with consonants are the most abundant type of stem ending. For words ending in a constant you simply add an 아 or an 어 to the end of the stem, depending on the vowel in the last syllable of the stem. If the vowel is an ㅏ or an ㅗ, add 아, and if it’s an ㅜ or an ㅓ, add 어.

Examples:

Dictionary English Stem Conjugate Form
먹다 to eat 먹 먹어
줏다 to die 죽 죽어
놀다 to play 놀 놀아
닫다 to close 닫 닫아
