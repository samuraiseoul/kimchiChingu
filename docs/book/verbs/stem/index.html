Verb and Adjective Stems | 連用形

In Korean, the easiest type of conjugation is the stem stem conjugation. It is the same for all verbs and adjectives, including the copula, 이다. Simply remove the 다 from the verb or adjective and you’re done.

Examples:

English Verb Dictionary Form Stem Form
to do 하다 하
to eat 먹다 먹
to be 이다 이
to use 쓰다 쓰
